Magic Kingdom

13/10/24

I was 8 years old when I travelled to Disney. I was so excited to see all the characters and try the attractions. On the first day, my family and I went to Magic Kingdom, I couldn't believe that I was standing in a Disney park! We spent all day meeting the characters and going to attractions. At midnight, we went to Magic Kingdom's castle and saw a firework show. It was a beautiful experience.

Animal Kingdom

15/10/24

On the third day, my family and I went to Animal Kingdom. My sister was the most excited in the family, since she loves all the things related to animals. I was excited too, but I don't think that more than she. We tried most of the attractions in the park, but the one I remember the most is Avatar. At first I was scared but as the attraction went on I got so excited. We also bought tickets for The Lion King show, and it was awesome. I believe it is in my top 5 favorite shows.

Hollywood Studios

18/10/24

On the fifth day, my family and I went to Hollywood Studios. I don't remember much about this theme park, but I do remember we saw an incredible show! All characters were there and there were also a lot of fireworks. We concluded this vacations so exhausted, yet we had so much fun.
